Community News Bulletin

New bike lanes approved for Main Street

The city council approved the construction of protected bike lanes along Main Street to improve safety and encourage sustainable transportation.

Funding will come from the regional transportation grant, with construction expected to start in early autumn.

Free youth coding workshops

Neighborhood learning centers will offer free after-school coding classes for middle and high school students beginning next month.

Registration is open online at the civic portal and space is limited to the first 30 students per location.
